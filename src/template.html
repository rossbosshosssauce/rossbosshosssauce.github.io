About

Driven by a relentless work ethic and a commitment to continuous learning, I leverage a versatile skill set to build high-performance web applications. As a front-end developer currently evolving into a full-stack engineer, I specialize in JavaScript and React, with extensive experience architecting solutions across Shopify, WordPress, and headless CMS platforms. I am passionate about the synergy of technical logic and creative problem-solving, always seeking new ways to optimize the user experience. When I’m not coding, I like to spend my time working out, seeing friends, and playing with my two chihuahuas.
